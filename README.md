## What I Do 🛠️

### 🖥️ Backend Development
I have more than **10 years of experience** in back-end development, with a strong focus on PHP (Laravel and Symfony). I’ve built scalable applications, APIs, and enterprise solutions from scratch, always prioritizing **clean architecture, performance, and security**. My experience also includes .NET (C#, VB.NET) and database design with SQL Server, MySQL, and MariaDB, giving me the ability to work across different technology stacks and environments.

### 🎨 Frontend Development
On the front end, I have worked extensively with **Angular, TypeScript, ReactJS, JavaScript, jQuery, and Bootstrap** to deliver responsive, modern, and user-friendly interfaces. I emphasize **pixel-perfect implementation** when converting designs into code, ensuring layouts and interactions are precise, consistent, and optimized for all devices.

### 🛠️ WordPress & Elementor
In recent years, I’ve specialized in WordPress development, particularly using **Elementor**. I regularly translate Figma designs into fully functional, responsive websites, with a strong focus on pixel-perfect accuracy and smooth user experience. I combine design fidelity with performance optimization to deliver sites that are both visually impressive and technically reliable.

### 🔌 Custom Plugins & Themes
Beyond design-driven work, I create **custom plugins and themes** for WordPress to meet unique business requirements. This includes adding functionality that off-the-shelf solutions don’t provide, integrating with third-party APIs, and extending WordPress to support specialized workflows. These tailored solutions help scale digital platforms while maintaining flexibility and performance.
